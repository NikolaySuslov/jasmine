DIRECTIONS

To control the helicopter, press 'z'. Keep the mouse over the joystick to control the Helicopter. To switch back out of control, hit 'z' again.

CONTROL KEYS

  • t = Forward
  • g = Backward
  • f = Strafe Left
  • h = Strafe Right
  • i = Up
  • k = Down
  • j = Rotate Left
  • l = Rotate Right
Press the space bar a few times if the helicopter needs to be reset.
